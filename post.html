APPLE RELEASES THEIR FIRST EXTENDABLE PHONE

REAL RETURN OF Y2K, SIDEKICKS!

Posted by Apple on December 1, 2022 20:00 ET

Apple is bringing back a blast from the past, Sidekicks! Known to be the phone that “knocked the world on its side,” alongside the upgraded wireless connectivity, this design is back with a twist. Firstly, the new name of the device is very true to all Apple products, iSide. The release date will be December 8th 2022.

Apple had bought the design and have worked together with T-Mobile to provide users with the best experience. The last update from T-Mobile being in 2018 with the 4G Data connection and limited text and call. Additionally, on April 6, 2019, T-Mobile made an announcement that Sidekicks no longer have accurate GPS navigation. Time to rescind that statement. iSide's now have unlimited data and an updated GPS location system. With the help of Apple’s IOS technology, location services are now working through the use of, “GPS and Bluetooth (where they're available), along with crowd-sourced Wi-Fi hotspots and cellular towers to determine the approximate location of your device,” according to Apple. Of course, your apps will not know your location unless prior permission is given.

The new features of the iSide. Alongside the upgrades to data usage and location services, the software of the formerly known Sidekick has had a complete remodel. IOS 16 is now available on iSides. This includes all the updates to enhanced facial recognition passcodes, iMessage updates and new emojis. iSide is adding on to easy texting, just flip it on its side and slide the screen up, you got easy typing. Users claim this new design for the formerly known sidekicks will be the best return from Y2K as of yet.

The Features

The iSide at 6.1 inches (diagonal) display with longer battery life and wireless charging capability. Also has multiple storage options from 128GB, 256GB and 512GB including iCloud options. It has True Tone and HDR display along with the support of multiple languages on screen to user preference. It contains an A16 Bionic chip similar to the iPhone 14. A dual-camera system which has portrait mode, portrait lighting, and night mode for great pictures of your night out.

iSide has multiple storage options. In this day and age having enough storage on your device is key to having a your life in the palm of your hands. Apple's iSide has this versatile feature.

Compared to the previous sidekicks, iSide has an upgraded GPS system which allow users to pinpoint accurate locations for their lost or stolen devices. Just login to iCloud server and locate your device.

Dual Camera System on the iSide allow for different camera settings and angles. There’s portrait mode, night mode, portrait lighting & many more additions.

"Details matter, it’s worth waiting to get it right." - Steve Jobs

The A16 Bionic Chip allows for your iSide’s efficiency cores to run 40% faster than any other phones in the market right now.

Preorder Now

You can preorder your iSide at any Apple location. Have any questions? Learn more about this product at apple.com.
